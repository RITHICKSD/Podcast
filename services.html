WHAT WE DO

Creator Services & Studio Booking

From renting acoustic booths to publishing feeds to major podcast networks. We handle the technical side so you can focus on writing scripts.

Physical Studio Rentals

Book soundproof rooms with engineers in-studio or connect remotely via IP codecs.

Studio A (Multi-Mic Setup)

$75 / hour

Our premier space. Perfect for up to 4 local speakers. Includes a dedicated sound engineer, Rodecaster Pro II board, Shure SM7B mics, and headphone monitors.

  • Multi-track recording (RAW WAV format)
  • In-room engineer assistance
  • Decoupled floating walls

Studio B (Solo & Co-Host)

$45 / hour

Optimized for solo creators, remote call interviews, or double co-hosts. High-quality acoustics, Neumann TLM103 mic, and Focusrite interface.

  • Ideal for remote guest interviews
  • High-speed fiber internet connection
  • Audio post-processing templates included

Global Distribution Pipeline

We push your audio files automatically to Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Amazon Music, and Overcast. Our custom server hosting features 99.9% uptime, fast bandwidth downloading, and instant transcription scripts.
