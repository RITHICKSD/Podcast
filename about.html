OUR STORY

The Audio Revolution

We started in 2021 as a collection of bedroom podcasters frustrated by corporate network algorithms. Today, we run three state-of-the-art facilities hosting 25 independent shows reaching millions of monthly ears.

Our Growth Milestone Timeline

How we transformed from an informal collective into a premium podcast studio network.

2022

First Combined Studio Session

Sarah and Marcus merge their separate bedroom podcasts under the "Resonate Network" label, recording on a simple four-channel mixer.

2023

1 Million Downloads Milestone

Three new shows join the network. We cross the 1,000,000 global download mark and move into a small office space in downtown.

2024

Acoustic Studio Buildout

Marcus designs Studio A with full decoupled walls, custom bass traps, and professional microphone layouts.

2025

Premium Subscription Launch

We build our ad-free RSS distribution software, launching the premium membership club for direct listener monetization.
